Kingdom Hearts

Kingdom Hearts is a video game series made by Square Enix in collaboration with Disney. It follows the adventures of a boy named Sora, who travels through several Disney and Final Fantasy themed worlds to save the universe.

The series has eight games in it so far, with five collections. The first game of the series was released in 2002, and the most recent installment in the series should be released sometime this year.
